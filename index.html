Our Story

Caddyshack Web Design was founded by Dominic Grebel and Jack Wolk, two lifelong friends who grew up as neighbors in St. Louis, Missouri. What started as childhood adventures in the neighborhood has evolved into a professional partnership dedicated to creating exceptional web experiences.

Our deep-rooted friendship and shared vision allow us to work seamlessly together, combining our unique skills and perspectives to deliver custom websites, mobile-optimized designs, and e-commerce solutions that not only look great but also drive real business results for companies across St. Louis and throughout Missouri.

We're proud to serve businesses in St. Louis, Clayton, Chesterfield, Kirkwood, Webster Groves, and surrounding areas, bringing the same dedication and creativity to every project that has defined our friendship for years. Whether you need a brand new website, a complete redesign, or ongoing maintenance and support, we're here to help your business succeed online.
